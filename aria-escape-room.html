Operation ARIA

An AI literacy escape room · 2–4 players · 90 minutes

⚡ MISSION BRIEFING

ARIA — the AI assistant at the heart of a major client engagement — has suffered a critical malfunction. Her language core is corrupted, her outputs are unreliable, and a high-stakes project proposal is due to land on a client's desk within the hour.

Your team has been assembled as the ARIA Recovery Unit: a cross-functional task force with one job — diagnose and repair ARIA's systems before the deadline expires.

Five chambers stand between you and a fully restored ARIA. Each chamber locks a critical system. Each system requires a different kind of expertise. No single player holds all the answers. You will need to share what you know, reason carefully, and agree as a team.

⚠️ The clock starts the moment you enter the first chamber. Work fast — but work smart.
